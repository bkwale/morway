# Morway Follow-Up Email Sequence

Use after sending the initial cold email. Space each follow-up 5-7 days apart.

---

## Follow-Up 1 (Day 7) — Gentle bump

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

I know inboxes get busy — happy to keep this brief. If 20 minutes is too much, even a 5-minute call to understand how your firm handles supplier invoices today would be valuable.

No pressure either way.

Best,
Walt

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

Hi [First Name],

## Follow-Up 3 (Day 21) — Last touch

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

Hi [First Name],

Last note from me — I don't want to be a nuisance.

If automating invoice processing isn't a priority for [Firm] right now, I completely understand. If it becomes one later, I'm at walt@morway.app.

---

## Notes

- **Attach one-pager** on Follow-Up 2 only (not all three)
- **Stop after Follow-Up 3** — don't send more than 3 total follow-ups
- **If they reply at any stage** — even "not interested" — respond personally and remove from sequence
- **If they reply with interest** — propose specific times for a 20-minute call, attach one-pager if not already sent
- **Track everything** in the Outreach Log tab of the prospect tracker
